After about 1 month of service, we also ask that new team members send a brief (<600 character) bio with 4-10 photos so that we can make an Instgram post introducing you and add your bio to the website!

You can see examples on our website [here](https://theunderdogfoundation.org/our-team).

A TUF operational member will reach out to you for your bio.
